# FAQ

## 1. Does this require connecting billing accounts?

No. The audit works using the AI tools, plans, and spend information you manually provide.

---

## 2. Is this only for large startups?

No. The tool is especially useful for small engineering teams where AI subscriptions accumulate informally over time.

---

## 3. Are the recommendations AI-generated?

The savings recommendations themselves use deterministic business logic for consistency and explainability. AI is only used to generate personalized summary text.

---

## 4. Why does the tool ask for email after the audit?

The audit is fully usable before email capture. Email is only requested if you want to save the report or receive future optimization updates.

---

## 5. What happens for high-savings audits?

If the audit identifies significant overspend, Credex may recommend discounted infrastructure credits or consultation opportunities to reduce recurring AI costs further.
